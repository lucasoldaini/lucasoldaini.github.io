Hi! I am Luca Soldaini, a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science at Georgetown University and a member of the Information Retrieval Lab. I was born and raised in Florence, Italy, where I earned my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Florence.

My research interests include text mining, information retrieval, and NLP applications for the medical domain. In particular, I am interested in improving access to medical information by bridging the knowledge gap between health information seekers and medical content. A copy of my resumé is available here.

My pronouns are he/him/his.
