ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Luca Soldaini and I'm a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Georgetown University. My area of research is text mining and information retrieval for the medical domain.

While I currently live in Washington, D.C., I grew up in Florence, Italy, where I earned my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering.

Besides information retrieval, I'm deeply interested in everything that pushes technology frontiers forward, human-computer interaction and tech industry dynamics. I'm also quite passionate about politics, human rights and I love to discuss design.

I enjoy freshly brewed black coffee, podcasts and running. I (infrequently) write on my blog on Tumblr. Pythonista by choice.
