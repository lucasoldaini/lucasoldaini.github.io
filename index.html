Luca Soldaini is a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science at Georgetown University and a member of the Information Retrieval Lab. He was born and raised in Florence, Italy, where he earned his undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering at University of Florence.

His research interests include text mining, information retrieval, and NLP applications for the medical domain. In particular, he is interested in improving access to medical information by bridging the knowledge gap between health information seekers and medical content.
