Luca Soldaini is a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Georgetown University. He was born and raised in Florence, Italy, where he earned his undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering at University of Florence.

Luca's research interests include text mining, information retrieval, and NLP applications for the medical domain. In particular, he is focused on improving access to medical information by bridging the knowledge gap between health information seekers and medical content.
